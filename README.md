<h2>What is Attack on Titan?</h2>
<p>
Attack on Titan (Japanese: 進撃の巨人, Shingeki no Kyojin) is a Japanese manga series written and illustrated by Hajime Isayama. 
It is set in a world where humanity lives inside cities surrounded by three enormous walls that protect them from gigantic man-eating humanoids called Titans.
</p>
<p>
The story follows Eren Yeager, his adopted sister Mikasa Ackerman, and their childhood friend Armin Arlert, 
who join the fight against the Titans after their hometown is destroyed and Eren's mother is eaten.
</p>

<h2>Story Summary</h2>
<p>
After the fall of Wall Maria, Eren vows to exterminate all Titans. 
He later discovers he can transform into a Titan himself and joins the Survey Corps to uncover the truth about the Titans, 
the walls, and the world beyond.
</p>
